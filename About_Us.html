Who We Are
My name is Victor Moreno. I am a proud owner of my Shih-Tzu puppy named Oso. When I first got Oso I drove from my home in Illinois to Pennsylvania to pick him up from a breeder there. It was a long journey, but ultimately worth it. I created this website because there are so much items in the market for dogs/puppies. I felt a bit overwhelmed. I wanted a place where I can find good recommended products that are safe and healthy for my Oso. I decided to add products that I personally like from the internet for new and experienced dog owners to get inspired and possibly purchase as well.
